# CNT-PIPELINE — Quy trình content từ seed tới ship

> **DRI:** Content Director + Producer
> **Status:** `APPROVED_FOR_DOCUMENTATION`

```text
Seed → Reference/Problem Card → Content Contract → Paper review
→ Graybox → Gameplay validation → Art/Audio production
→ Integration → Quality/performance/localization review → Release candidate
```

Không sản xuất hero asset trước khi grammar/lesson được graybox chứng minh. Mỗi content instance có DRI, domain owner, stable ID, dependencies, budget, acceptance, fixtures và replacement/migration policy.

## Definition of Done

Gameplay distinctness, fairness, technical validation, performance tier, original asset review, localization, accessibility cues, audio/VFX budget, save/reload, debug hooks, telemetry và regression đều đạt gate. “Có trong game” không đồng nghĩa content done.
